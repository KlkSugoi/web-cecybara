What is the Past Perfect?

The pluperfect past tense, or antecopreterite in some countries, indicates a past action that occurred before another also past, that is, before another past tense. It is a relative tense of perfective aspect. It is characteristic of Romance languages.

In this article you will find useful information to know or reinforce your practice in the past perfect in English. You will discover its structures (affirmative, negative and interrogative), functions, complement with just, common errors and example sentences so that you can see its possibilities.
