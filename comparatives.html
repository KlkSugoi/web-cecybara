Comparatives

Comparatives are used to compare two people, animals, things, places, etc. The first thing we need is to know what we are referring to when we talk about comparison. The following example will help you understand it. Example:

A tiger is faster than a turtle.



This structure is used to express a greater or lesser quality of a thing, person, etc. with respect to another.
